About the Office of Business Affairs (OBA)

The Office of Business Affairs (OBA) at Pampanga State Agricultural University oversees diverse revenue-generating activities across the campus. From agricultural produce to rental properties, OBA is the backbone of the university's commercial and trading operations.

To align with the standards of public higher education institutions, the OBA System was developed to centralize and automate sales monitoring, inventory control, and financial operations into one secure, real-time platform.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, transparent, and technology-driven management of all revenue-generating projects at Pampanga State Agricultural University, ensuring accurate financial reporting and sustainable business operations that contribute to the university's growth.

Our Vision

To be a premier, self-sustaining business division within the university system, recognized for its automated processes, accountability, and outstanding contribution to the agricultural and commercial sectors of the region.

Why the OBA System Matters

Financial Transparency

Automated calculation and COA-compliant reporting ensures every peso is tracked, accounted for, and correctly attributed to the right business unit.

Real-Time Operations

From stock deductions to sales logging, all operations reflect instantly across the network, ending the era of manual, delayed record-keeping.

Secure Accountability

Role-based access controls and a strict audit trail mean the administration always knows who recorded what, and when.
